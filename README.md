# LandLink – Online Land Buyer-Seller Platform

## **Project Description**
LandLink is a web-based platform that connects land buyers and sellers efficiently. Buyers can search for land plots based on preferences such as location, area, price, and land type. Sellers can list their properties, making it easier to reach potential buyers. This platform simplifies land transactions by providing a one-stop solution for property listings and searches.

---

## **Features**
- **User Registration & Login** – Secure authentication for buyers and sellers.
- **Property Listing** – Sellers can upload land details including images, price, and location.
- **Search & Filter** – Buyers can filter listings based on location, price range, area, and land type.
- **Responsive Design** – Fully functional on desktop and mobile devices.
- **Contact Sellers** – Buyers can contact sellers directly through the platform.
- **Interactive Map (Optional)** – Visual representation of available lands.
